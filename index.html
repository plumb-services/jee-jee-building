Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our plumbing and heating services.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Alaska with a focus on the surrounding areas of our location. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific area.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed and insured. We meet all Alaska state requirements for plumbing and HVAC services.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency plumbing and heating services. Just call our emergency line at (907) 209-6648 at any time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and cash. We also offer financing options for larger projects - ask us for details.

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

For emergency calls, we typically respond within 2 hours. For non-emergency service calls, we schedule appointments within 24-48 hours.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer affordable maintenance plans for both plumbing and HVAC systems to help prevent costly repairs and extend the life of your systems.
